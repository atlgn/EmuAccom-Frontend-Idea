Alfam Dormitory 2+1 House Dorm

Prices Starting From 3 Month / $1000 or 6 Month / $1500

Contact Now

          1+1 deluxe double rooms attract attention with spacious and wide environment. The separation of the bedroom and the living area and the kitchen area create private spaces for the individuals living together. Differences in working and sleeping hours of students may cause conflicts in single rooms. In order to prevent the occurrence of such situations, two different sections were created in double deluxe rooms to allow two people to easily agree. While one of the students is sleeping the other one can work comfortably, watch TV or use the kitchen. The double rooms are designed as 1+1 and it is the most important difference of Alfam.

Alfam Dormitory student dormitory has many extra features such as 24-hour reception service, laundry, dryer machines, car parking, lobby service, elevator, closed-circuit security camera, generator and sound-heat insulation besides the room features mentioned below.
The double rooms having separate bedrooms have their own kitchens. The students can cook their own meals in the kitchen with built-in cooker, hood and mini-fridge. 1+1 double deluxe rooms stand out as the most preferred accommodation option for the students due to its affordable price and comfort.
